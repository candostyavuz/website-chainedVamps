TOKENOMICS

Avalanche Academy uses an all new reflectionary minting system that earns ape holders AVAX! This means that 10% of every minting fee is reflected back to existing AVAX Apes holders and can be claimed at any time! The rewards don't stop when minting is complete! In our marketplace original minters of AVAX Apes earn 1% royalties each time their AVAX Ape is resold and 3% of each sale is redistributed between all AVAX Ape holders.
